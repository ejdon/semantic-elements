The Best Shops and Restaurants in Langley, Washington

Who said island life has to be boring? There are lots of great places to check out on Whidbey Island in Washington State. Specifically, we will be looking at Langely, an ocean-side town full of amazing shops and restaurants.

We will cover:

Restaurants

  • Village Pizzeria
  • Useless Bay Cafe

Shops

  • Edit
  • The Star Store

Restaurants

Village Pizzeria

Located right on the water, they have some of the best pizza in town. They have both indoor and outdoor seating but they don't take reservations so plan to wait if you go during peak hours. However, the wait is worth it!

They also offer take-and-bake, and if you visit a local grocery store, you may see some in the refrigerated section! Pro tip: try the pesto pizza!

Contact Information:
  • Address: 106 1st St, Langley, WA 98260
  • Phone Number: 360-221-3363
  • Pet-friendly outdoor seating

Useless Bay Cafe

Useless Bay Cafe is the best spot in town for coffee. Their kitchen is also open until 3PM and they have a great menu. Another pro tip: try the Sweet Bee Latte!

They have plenty of great outdoor seating and, if you're lucky, you'll spot one of the many adorable bunnies that live in Langely. Great spot for dog watching as well!

Useless Bay Cafe is named after a bay on Whidbey Island on Western side of the island. A great place for a picnic, you can check out more informaiton here.

Contact Information:
  • Address: 121 2nd St, Langley, WA 98260
  • Phone Number: 360-221-4515
  • Pet-friendly outdoor seating

Shops

Edit

Edit is a cute boutique on the main street in Langely. The owner, Holly, finds local goods from around the world and brings back her nifty finds.

Edit is a great place to find linen clothes and accessories and modern home goods. Warning: you'll most likely leave with something you didn't expect to buy!

Contact Information:
  • Address: 306 1st St, Langley, WA 98260
  • Phone Number: 360-381-0045
  • Fitting rooms available

The Star Store

The Star Store is a local favorite on the island. A combination of a clothing store and a grocery store, you're likely to find anything you need here (including a pre-made Village Pizzeria pizza!)

On the rooftop, you'll find Prima Bistro, a restaurant that overlooks the town and has a view of the water.

Contact Information:
  • Address: 201 1st St, Langley, WA 98260
  • Phone Number: 360-221-5223
